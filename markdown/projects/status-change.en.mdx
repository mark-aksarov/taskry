## Status Change

The system provides two types of status changes: <b>single</b> and <b>bulk</b>.

---

### Single Status Change

To change a project status, click the <b>“Actions Menu”</b> button (three-dot icon) on the right side of the card or list item, and select a new project status from the menu that appears.

After selection, the status will be updated for the chosen project.

---

### Bulk Status Change

To change the status of multiple projects at once, select the desired projects using the checkboxes. Then click the <b>“Actions”</b> button and choose a new status for the selected projects.
