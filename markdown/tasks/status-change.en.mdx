## Status change

The system supports two types of status changes: <b>single</b> and <b>bulk</b>.

---

### Single status change

To change the status of a task, go to the <b>"Tasks"</b> page, click the <b>"Actions Menu"</b> button (three dots icon) on the right side of a card or list item, and select a new task status from the dropdown menu.

After selection, the status will be updated for the chosen task.

---

### Bulk status change

To change the status of multiple tasks at once, select the required tasks using checkboxes. Then click the <b>"Actions"</b> button and choose a new status for the tasks.
