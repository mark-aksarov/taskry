## Projects

Project management is a core feature of Taskry that helps you organize team collaboration.

---

### What is a Project?

A project is a container for all your tasks, team members, and materials related to a specific initiative. Each project has its own settings, access permissions, and activity history.

---

### Creating a Project

Create new projects in just a few clicks:

#### 1. Open the Projects Panel

Go to the main menu and select "My Projects".

#### 2. Click "Create Project"

Enter the name and description of your project.

#### 3. Choose a Template or Type

Taskry offers various templates for different project types.

---

### Managing Project Members

Add and remove project members through settings. You can assign different roles: administrator, editor, or viewer.
