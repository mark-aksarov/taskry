## Introduction

Welcome to the Taskry documentation — a modern task manager designed for efficient team collaboration.

---

### What is Taskry?

Taskry is a comprehensive solution for project and task management, built for teams of any size. Our platform brings together planning, communication, and analytics tools in a single interface.

With Taskry, you can easily organize workflows, track progress, and achieve your goals faster.

---

### Quick Start

Start using Taskry in just a few minutes. Follow these simple steps.

#### 1. Create an account

Go to the sign-up page and create an account using your email, or sign in with Google, GitHub, or Microsoft.

#### 2. Your first project

After signing in, click “Create Project” and choose a template that best fits your team.

#### 3. Invite your team

Invite your colleagues via email or share a link to join the project.
