## Company actions

Working with companies includes the following actions:

- Create company — <span className="font-normal">add a new company</span>
- Edit company — <span className="font-normal">change the name of an existing company</span>
- Delete company — <span className="font-normal">remove a company from the system</span>

---

### Creating a company

To create a company, go to the <b>“Companies”</b> page, click the <b>“Create new”</b> button at the top of the page, and in the modal window enter the company name.

#### Editing a company

To edit a company, in the <b>“Actions menu”</b> select <b>“Edit”</b>. After that, an edit window will open where you can change the company name. The company name cannot be empty.

To delete a company, in the <b>“Actions menu”</b> select <b>“Delete”</b>. In the modal window that appears, click the <b>“Delete”</b> button.
