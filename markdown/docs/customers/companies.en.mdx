## Companies

A company is an entity that represents an organization associated with a customer and is used for grouping customers.

---

### Viewing companies

Companies are displayed in dropdown lists when creating or editing a customer. They are also available on the Companies page and used for filtering.

To view all companies, go to the <b>Customers</b> page, click the <b>Management</b> button at the top of the page, and select <b>Companies</b> from the menu to navigate to the <b>Companies</b> page.

## Company actions

Working with companies includes the following actions:

- Create company — <span className="font-normal">adding a new company</span>
- Edit company — <span className="font-normal">modifying the name of an existing company</span>
- Delete company — <span className="font-normal">removing a company from the system</span>

---

### Adding a company

To add a company, go to the <b>Companies</b> page, click the <b>Create new</b> button at the top of the page, and enter the company name in the modal window.
