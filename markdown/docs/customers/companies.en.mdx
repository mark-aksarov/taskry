## Companies

A company is an entity that represents an organization linked to a customer and is used to group customers.

---

### Viewing companies

Companies are shown in dropdown lists when you create or edit a customer. They are also available on the companies page and used for filtering.

To view all companies, go to the <b>“Customers”</b> page, click the <b>“Manage”</b> button at the top of the page, and select <b>“Companies”</b> from the menu to open the <b>“Companies”</b> page.
