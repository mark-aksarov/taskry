## Customer filters

To filter customers, go to the <b>"Customers"</b> page and click the <b>"Filters"</b> button at the top of the page. A filter modal window will open, where the following filters are available:

- Has no active projects — <span className="font-normal">shows only customers without active projects.</span>
- Has active projects — <span className="font-normal">shows only customers who have active projects.</span>
- Has overdue projects — <span className="font-normal">shows only customers with overdue projects.</span>
- Company — <span className="font-normal">filters customers by company.</span>
