## Customer sorting

To sort customers, go to the <b>"Customers"</b> page and click the <b>"Sort"</b> button at the top of the page. In the opened menu, the following sorting options are available:

- By full name — <span className="font-normal">sorts customers by full name.</span>
- By company — <span className="font-normal">sorts customers by company.</span>
