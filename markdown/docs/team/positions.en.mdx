## Position

A position is an entity that defines a user’s position and is used to describe the user.

---

### Viewing positions

Positions are shown in a dropdown list when you edit a user. They are also available on the positions page and used for filtering.

To view all positions, go to the <b>“Positions”</b> page, click the <b>“Manage”</b> button at the top of the page, and select <b>“Positions”</b> from the menu to open the <b>“Positions”</b> page.
