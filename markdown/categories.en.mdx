## Categories

Categories help you organize and classify your projects and tasks.

---

### What is a Category?

A category is a way to group and organize tasks by type, topic, or department. This simplifies navigation and searching for needed tasks.

---

### Creating a Category

Organize your work using categories:

#### 1. Open Project Settings

Go to "Settings" → "Task Categories".

#### 2. Click "New Category"

Enter the name and choose a color for identification.

#### 3. Add a Description

Describe what types of tasks this category is for.

---

### Using Categories

Assign categories when creating or editing tasks. Filter tasks by categories for quick search.
