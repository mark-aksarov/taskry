## Comments

Comments are an important tool for communication within Taskry between team members.

---

### Purpose of Comments

Comments allow you to discuss tasks, share ideas, and coordinate actions directly in the context of work. All discussions remain attached to the corresponding task.

---

### Adding Comments

Participate in task discussions:

#### 1. Open a Task

Select the required task to open its details.

#### 2. Scroll to the "Comments" Section

Find the field to add a comment.

#### 3. Enter Text and Send

Write your comment and click "Send".

---

### Comment Notifications

Receive notifications when someone replies to your comment or mentions you in a discussion.
