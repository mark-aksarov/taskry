## Deleting a client

The system supports two types of deletion: <b>single</b> and <b>bulk</b>. Both options allow you to permanently remove clients from the system. Client deletion is irreversible, so before deleting, make sure the client is no longer needed and all important data has been saved.

---

### Single deletion

To delete a client, go to the <b>"Clients"</b> page, click the <b>"Actions menu"</b> button (three dots icon) on the right side of the card or list item, and select <b>"Delete"</b> from the dropdown menu.

### Bulk deletion

To delete multiple clients at once, select the required clients using checkboxes. Then click the <b>"Actions"</b> button at the top of the page and choose <b>"Delete"</b>.
