## Project editing

To edit a project, click the <b>“Actions menu”</b> button (the three-dot icon) on the right side of the project card or list item, and select <b>“Edit”</b> from the dropdown menu.

![image](https://storage.yandexcloud.net/task-manager-docs/project_editing_en.jpg)

After that, an editing window will open where you can change the project name, deadline, description, status, category, and client. Once saved, the updated data will be immediately reflected in the project list.
