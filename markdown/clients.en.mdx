## Clients

Managing client information and their projects helps you organize work with external partners.

---

### What is a Client?

A client is an external organization or person for whom you perform work. In Taskry, you can track all projects and tasks associated with each client.

---

### Adding a Client

Manage client information:

#### 1. Open the "Clients" Section

Go to the menu and select "Clients".

#### 2. Click "Add Client"

Enter the name, contact information, and other details.

#### 3. Link Projects

Select or create projects for this client.

---

### Tracking Client Projects

View all tasks and work progress for each client. Generate reports on completed work.
