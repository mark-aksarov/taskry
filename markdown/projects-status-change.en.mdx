## Status change

The system provides two types of status updates: <b>single</b> and <b>bulk</b>.

---

### Single status change

To change the status of a project, click the <b>“Action menu”</b> button (three dots icon) on the right side of the project card or list item, and select a new project status from the dropdown menu.

![image](https://storage.yandexcloud.net/task-manager-docs/project_single_status_change_en.jpg)

After selection, the status will be updated for the chosen project.

---

### Bulk status change

To change the status of multiple projects at once, select the required projects using checkboxes. Then click the <b>“Actions”</b> button and choose a new status for the projects.

![image](https://storage.yandexcloud.net/task-manager-docs/project_multiple_status_change_en.jpg)

After selection, the status will be updated for the selected projects.
